AKAN NAME CALCULATOR


This is a web-application that allows users to know the day they were born. It is achieved when the users input their date of birth. The web-application will then output the Akan name depending on the gender of the users.

Akan names are names derived in the Ghanian culture were by a child is named according to the day of the week they were born and gender. They include

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi Monday: Kwadwo Tuesday: Kwabena Wednesday: Kwaku Thursday: Yaw Friday: Kofi Saturday: Kwame

    • Female

  • Sunday: Akosua Monday: Adwoa Tuesday: Abenaa Wednesday: Akua Thursday: Yaa Friday: Afua Saturday: Ama
